HYDRA | HYdrological Drought & Rainfall Atlas
ENSO PDO IOD AMO NAO SAM NAM PNA

A Python toolkit for exploring the links between climate variability modes and hydroclimate fields. Analyze lagged regressions, visualize the connections between different modes of climate variability and global drought and rainfall patterns.

Key Features

Lagged Regression

Capture seasonal and interannual lags between climate indices and hydroclimate anomalies.

Flexible Inputs

Works seamlessly with xarray seasonal datasets (DJF, MAM, JJA, SON).

Custom Plotting

Seasonal maps and multi-panel plots with clean color schemes.

Method

HYdrological Drought & Rainfall Atlas

Regression of ENSO onto global PDSI, with hatching for non-significant areas.
