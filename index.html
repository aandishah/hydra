HYDRA

HYdrological Drought & Rainfall Atlas

A Python toolkit for exploring the links between climate variability modes (ENSO, PDO, AMO, IOD) and hydroclimate fields (PDSI, SST, soil moisture). Analyze lagged regressions, visualize teleconnections, and uncover global drought patterns.
